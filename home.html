About Us

Welcome to Aurorian Radio 93.3, the station where the heartbeats of Auroria meet the rhythms of the world! Founded on August 31, 2024, by Bradaski and AdvencherFrogYT, this station has quickly become a cornerstone of Aurorian culture and music.

Aurorian Radio 93.3 brings you a unique mix of genres ranging from classic rock, pop, and electronic, to traditional Aurorian folk. We pride ourselves on showcasing the best of both Aurorian and English language music, making us the perfect destination for music lovers everywhere.

Our Mission

Our mission is to bridge the gap between Auroria and the rest of the world through the universal language of music. We believe that music has the power to connect people, no matter the language or genre. Tune in and discover the diverse soundscape that defines Aurorian Radio 93.3.
